Interactive planning tools

Executive calculators for revenue, recovery, and operational opportunity.

These calculators are designed as planning tools for healthcare leaders evaluating financial leakage, patient payment recovery, A/R performance, and telehealth revenue potential. Results are illustrative and should be used to support assessment discussions, not as final financial projections.

Revenue planning tool

Revenue Leakage Calculator

Estimate the annual impact of rejected claims and unrecovered claim value relative to a more disciplined outsourced-state benchmark.

Modeled benchmark: 1% rejected claims and 3% unrecovered rejected-claim value in a higher-performance outsourced-state scenario.
Patient payment planning tool

Patient Payment Recovery Calculator

Estimate how stronger payment structures and financing pathways may improve recovery of patient balances.

Modeled benchmark assumes stronger payment options can materially improve recovery of otherwise under-collected balances.
A/R planning tool

A/R Recovery Calculator

Estimate current collections from past-due accounts and compare them to a stronger modeled recovery scenario.

Modeled benchmark compares current-state performance to a 45% recovery scenario while accounting for collection costs.
Growth planning tool

Telehealth Revenue Estimator

Model annual telehealth revenue using provider count, expected visits per day, and average billable value per visit.

Estimate assumes 260 work days annually. Use conservative assumptions for planning discipline.
